Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I hit my 20 view limit on the Starter plan?

Our free Starter plan has a limit of 20 views per member (views are counted in aggregate of company and discovery views). To keep going past 20 views, you will need to upgrade to a Premium plan.

If you want to stay in the same Workspace as your team and upgrade, you can move your entire team to a Professional Workspace without paying for everyone.

What's the difference between a free and paid user?

The primary difference between a free and a paid user is the level of access granted. Free users can access a limited number of companies. Users with a paid seat unlock access to unlimited companies and contact information.

How do searches work?

All of our plans include discovery searches. If you want to discover new companies, we'll allow you to use filters to find companies that match what you are looking for.

Where does Kona Equity data come from?

We use proprietary algorithms to generate our business health data. We crawl millions of sources as well as partner and license data from outside 3rd parties.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Please email admin@konaequity.com if you need to cancel your subscription.

Who should try Premium?

Kona Equity is used most commonly by business development teams, sales representatives, business owners, marketers, private equity firms, corporate development teams, and investment bankers.
